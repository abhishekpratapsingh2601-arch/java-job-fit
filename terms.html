JavaJobFit

Terms

JavaJobFit provides ATS-style estimates and resume improvement suggestions for Java developers. The app is an informational tool, not a hiring decision system.

No Guarantees

JavaJobFit does not guarantee interviews, recruiter responses, ATS success, job offers, hiring outcomes, salary outcomes, or employment decisions.

User Responsibility

You are responsible for reviewing suggestions before using them. Only add skills, experience, projects, and achievements that are truthful and that you can defend in an interview.

Payments

Paid plans are currently marked as coming soon or early access. JavaJobFit does not process real payments until an actual payment provider is configured.
